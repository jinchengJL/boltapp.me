Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the Bolt - Compiler Explorer iOS app.

Our Commitment to Privacy

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information, user data from your use of the app.

To provide its core functionality, the app acts as a client for the Compiler Explorer service at godbolt.org. When you compile code within the app, the source code and selected compiler options are sent directly to the godbolt.org servers for processing. Please refer to the privacy policy on the website for more details.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, feel free to contact us at support@boltapp.me.
